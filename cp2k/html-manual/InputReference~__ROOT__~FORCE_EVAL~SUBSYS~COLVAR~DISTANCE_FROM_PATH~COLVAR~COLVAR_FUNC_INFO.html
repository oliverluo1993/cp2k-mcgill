Section COLVAR_FUNC_INFO

Index of all sections. This section is located at __ROOT__%FORCE_EVAL%SUBSYS%COLVAR%DISTANCE_FROM_PATH%COLVAR%COLVAR_FUNC_INFO.

Specify further data possibly used by colvars, depending on the starting geometry, for computing the functions value. This section can not be repeated.
