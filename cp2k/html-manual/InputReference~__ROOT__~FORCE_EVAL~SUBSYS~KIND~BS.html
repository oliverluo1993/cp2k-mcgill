Section BS

Index of all sections. This section is located at __ROOT__%FORCE_EVAL%SUBSYS%KIND%BS.

Define the required atomic orbital occupation assigned in initialization of the density matrix, by adding or subtracting electrons from specific angular momentum channels. It works only with GUESS ATOMIC. This section can not be repeated.
