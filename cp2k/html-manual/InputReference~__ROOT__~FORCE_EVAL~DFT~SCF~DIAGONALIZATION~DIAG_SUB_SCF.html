Section DIAG_SUB_SCF

Index of all sections. This section is located at __ROOT__%FORCE_EVAL%DFT%SCF%DIAGONALIZATION%DIAG_SUB_SCF.

Activation of self-consistenf subspace refinement by diagonalization of H by adjusting the occupation but keeping the MOS unchanged. This section can not be repeated.
