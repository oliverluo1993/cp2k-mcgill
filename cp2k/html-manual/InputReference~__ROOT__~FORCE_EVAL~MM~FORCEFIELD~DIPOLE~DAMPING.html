Section DAMPING

Index of all sections. This section is located at __ROOT__%FORCE_EVAL%MM%FORCEFIELD%DIPOLE%DAMPING.

This section specifies optional electric field damping for the polarizable atoms. This section can be repeated.

Subsections

None
