Section HISTORY

Index of all sections. This section is located at __ROOT__%SWARM%GLOBAL_OPT%HISTORY.

Section controling the history of visited minima and how minima are recognized at a later point. This section can not be repeated.

Subsections

None

Section keywords

  • ENERGY_PRECISION
  • FINGERPRINT_PRECISION

Keyword descriptions

ENERGY_PRECISION
If the difference of two energies is below this threshold they are considert equal.
This keyword expects a real. Default value: 1.0000000000000001E-05
FINGERPRINT_PRECISION
If the euclidean distance of two fingerprints is below this threshold, they are considert equal.
This keyword expects a real. Default value: 1.0000000000000000E-02
