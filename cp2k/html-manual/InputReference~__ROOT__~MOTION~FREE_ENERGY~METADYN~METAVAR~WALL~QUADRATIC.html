DIRECTION
TYPE (WALL_PLUS|WALL_MINUS)
Specify the direction of the wall.
This keyword expects a keyword. Default value: WALL_PLUS
valid keywords:
  • WALL_PLUS: Wall extends from the position towards larger values of COLVAR
  • WALL_MINUS: Wall extends from the position towards smaller values of COLVAR
K
K
Specify the value of the quadratic potential constant: K*(CV-POS)^2
This keyword expects a real. Default unit: [hartree].
