Section CENTER_COORDINATES

Index of all sections. This section is located at __ROOT__%FORCE_EVAL%SUBSYS%TOPOLOGY%CENTER_COORDINATES.

Allows centering the coordinates of the system in the box. The centering point can be defined by the user. This section can not be repeated.
