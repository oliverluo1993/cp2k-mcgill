Section RESPA

Index of all sections. This section is located at __ROOT__%MOTION%MD%RESPA.

Multiple timestep integration based on RESPA (implemented for NVE only). RESPA exploits multiple force_eval. In this case the order of the force_eval maps the order of the respa shells from the slowest to the fastest force evaluation. If force_evals share the same subsys, it's enough then to specify the subsys in the force_eval corresponding at the first index in the multiple_force_eval list. Can be used to speedup classical and ab initio MD simulations. This section can not be repeated.

This section cites following references: [TUCKERMAN1992] [Guidon2008]
