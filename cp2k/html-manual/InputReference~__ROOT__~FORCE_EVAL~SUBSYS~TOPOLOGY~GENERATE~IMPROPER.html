Section IMPROPER

Index of all sections. This section is located at __ROOT__%FORCE_EVAL%SUBSYS%TOPOLOGY%GENERATE%IMPROPER.

Section used to add/remove improper in the connectivity. Useful for systems with a complex connectivity, difficult to find out automatically. This section can be repeated.
