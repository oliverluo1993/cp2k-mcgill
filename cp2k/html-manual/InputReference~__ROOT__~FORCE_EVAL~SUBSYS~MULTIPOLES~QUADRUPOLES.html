Section QUADRUPOLES

Index of all sections. This section is located at __ROOT__%FORCE_EVAL%SUBSYS%MULTIPOLES%QUADRUPOLES.

Specifies the quadrupoles of the particles. This section can not be repeated.

_DEFAULT_KEYWORD_
{Real} {Real} {Real} {Real} {Real} {Real}
The quadrupole components for each atom in the format:

Qxx Qxy Qxz Qyy Qyz Qzz
This keyword expects precisely 6 reals, and may repeat
