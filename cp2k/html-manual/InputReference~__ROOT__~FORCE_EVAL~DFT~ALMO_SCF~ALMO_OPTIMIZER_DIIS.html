Section ALMO_OPTIMIZER_DIIS

Index of all sections. This section is located at __ROOT__%FORCE_EVAL%DFT%ALMO_SCF%ALMO_OPTIMIZER_DIIS.

Controls the iterative DIIS-accelerated optimization of block-diagonal ALMOs. This section can not be repeated.
