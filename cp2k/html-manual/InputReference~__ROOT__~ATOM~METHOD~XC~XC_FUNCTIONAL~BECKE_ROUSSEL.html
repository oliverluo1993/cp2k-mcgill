Section BECKE_ROUSSEL

Index of all sections. This section is located at __ROOT__%ATOM%METHOD%XC%XC_FUNCTIONAL%BECKE_ROUSSEL.

Becke Roussel exchange hole model. Can be usedas long range correction with a truncated coulomb potential. This section can not be repeated.
