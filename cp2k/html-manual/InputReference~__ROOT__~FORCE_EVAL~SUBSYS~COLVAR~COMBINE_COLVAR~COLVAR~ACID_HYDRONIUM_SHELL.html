Section ACID_HYDRONIUM_SHELL

Index of all sections. This section is located at __ROOT__%FORCE_EVAL%SUBSYS%COLVAR%COMBINE_COLVAR%COLVAR%ACID_HYDRONIUM_SHELL.

Section to define the dissociation of a carboxylic acid in water. Number of oxygens in the 1st shell of the hydronium. Equation (3) in Supplementary Info of J. Am. Chem. Soc.,128, 2006, 11318. Similar to the HYDRONIUM colvar, but with modification for the acid. This section can not be repeated.
