Section QUADRUPOLE

Index of all sections. This section is located at __ROOT__%FORCE_EVAL%MM%FORCEFIELD%QUADRUPOLE.

This section specifies that we will perform an SCF quadrupole calculation of the MM atoms. Needs KEYWORD POL_SCF in POISSON secton. This section can be repeated.

Subsections

None
