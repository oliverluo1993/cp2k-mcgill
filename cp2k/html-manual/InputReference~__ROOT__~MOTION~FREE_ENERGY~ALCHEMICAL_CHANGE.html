Section ALCHEMICAL_CHANGE

Index of all sections. This section is located at __ROOT__%MOTION%FREE_ENERGY%ALCHEMICAL_CHANGE.

Controls the calculation of delta free energies with the alchemical change method. This section can not be repeated.
