Section WALL

Index of all sections. This section is located at __ROOT__%MOTION%FREE_ENERGY%METADYN%METAVAR%WALL.

Controls the activation of walls on COLVAR during a metadynamic run. This section can be repeated.

Subsections

  • REFLECTIVE
  • QUADRATIC
  • QUARTIC
  • GAUSSIAN

Section keywords

  • TYPE
  • POSITION

Keyword descriptions

TYPE
TYPE (REFLECTIVE|QUADRATIC|QUARTIC|GAUSSIAN|NONE)
Specify the type of wall
This keyword expects a keyword. Default value: NONE
valid keywords:
  • REFLECTIVE: Reflective wall. Colvar velocity is inverted when the colvar is beyond the wall position.
  • QUADRATIC: Applies a quadratic potential at the wall position.
  • QUARTIC: Applies a quartic potential at the wall position.
  • GAUSSIAN: Applies a gaussian potential at the wall position.
  • NONE: No walls are applied.
POSITION
POSITION
Specify the value of the colvar for the wall position
This keyword expects a real. Default unit: [internal_cp2k].
