Section FREE_ENERGY

Index of all sections. This section is located at __ROOT__%MOTION%FREE_ENERGY.

Controls the calculation of free energy and free energy derivatives with different possible methods. This section can not be repeated.

METHOD
METHOD (METADYN|UI|AC)
Defines the method to use to compute free energy.
This keyword expects a keyword. Default value: METADYN
valid keywords:
  • METADYN: Metadynamics
  • UI: Umbrella Integration
  • AC: Alchemical Change
